Investment Philosophy

How We Think

01 — Quality
3 Great Over 7 Mediocre

Today's build proves the principle: 7 candidates screened, 3 bought, 4 passed on. Every holding earns its place with ≥ 8/10 conviction, ≥ 3 agent BUY votes, and a clearly articulable long-term thesis. We never buy to deploy — we buy to own.

02 — Discipline
Margin of Safety Always

All 3 positions entered with ≥ 20% margin of safety. CRWD and VRT were rejected despite compelling narratives — the math didn't support a 2× risk-reward. Expected upside must be at least 2× the estimated downside before a single dollar moves.

03 — Survival
Survive to Compound

$400 cash held deliberately at the 40% ceiling — not because we ran out of ideas, but because the rules prohibited forcing capital into overvalued names. Missing CRWD at current prices is acceptable. A permanent loss from buying at 93× forward earnings is not.
